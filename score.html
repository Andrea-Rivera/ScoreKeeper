SCORING GAME

This App helps you to keet the track of your scores when you are playing. In this App is only allowed 2 players.

  1. The maximum score will be set in the box with name "Input number"
  2. Then each participant will click on "Player One" or "Player two" each time they score.
  3. The player which reaches the maximum score first will be the winner!!
